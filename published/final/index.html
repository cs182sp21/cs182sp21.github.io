Spring 2021 CS W182 / 282A at UC Berkeley

Final Project

Thursday, May 11, 3-6 p.m.

Logistics

  • Date and Time: Thursday, May 11, 3-6 p.m.
  • Locations:
    • Evans 10 (Sections 101-107)
    • Pauley Ballroom (Sections 108-124)
  • 3 sheets (front and back) of hand written notes are allowed.
  • Bring your Cal ID.
  • No calculators are allowed.

Topics

The exam is cumulative, with approximately double the number of questions on post-midterm material.

Preparation

During dead week, the staff will host events during regular lecture times.

  • Review Lecture Tuesday, May 2, 12:30 - 2 p.m. in Pauley Ballroom
  • Guerrilla Section Wednesday, May 3, 2-5 p.m. in Woz Lounge
  • Problem-Based Review Thursday, May 4, 12:30 - 2 p.m. in Pauley Ballroom
